## NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (Recruitment / Interview Process)

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**[Company Name]**, a [jurisdiction] corporation with its principal place of business at [address] ("Company"), and

**[Candidate Name]**, an individual ("Candidate").

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Company wishes to share certain Confidential Information with the Candidate during the recruitment and interview process for the position of **[Position Title]** (the "Purpose"). This Agreement protects information shared during interviews, assessments, and related discussions.

### 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed to the Candidate during the recruitment process, including but not limited to:

- Role details, responsibilities, and team structure
- Compensation structures, benefits, and equity programs
- Organizational charts and reporting lines
- Product roadmaps or strategies shared during interviews
- Technical challenges or business problems presented during assessments
- Names and identities of other candidates or interviewers (unless publicly known)
- Any information identified as confidential during the interview process

### 3. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does not include:

(a) Information that is publicly available or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Candidate;
(b) Information the Candidate already knows from a non-confidential source;
(c) General knowledge and experience that the Candidate possesses independently.

### 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE CANDIDATE

The Candidate shall:

(a) Use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating the employment opportunity;
(b) Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party;
(c) Not use the Confidential Information for the benefit of any current or future employer;
(d) Return or destroy all materials containing Confidential Information upon request or upon conclusion of the recruitment process.

### 5. NO EMPLOYMENT OBLIGATION

This Agreement does not constitute an offer of employment, a guarantee of future employment, or any commitment by the Company to the Candidate.

### 6. NO NON-COMPETE

Nothing in this Agreement restricts the Candidate's ability to seek or accept employment with any other organization, or to continue in the Candidate's current employment.

### 7. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **[Term: 1 year]** from the Effective Date, regardless of whether the Candidate is hired. The confidentiality obligations shall automatically expire at the end of the Term.

### 8. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon conclusion of the recruitment process, the Candidate shall return or destroy all documents, files, and materials containing Confidential Information, including any notes taken during interviews.

### 9. REMEDIES

The Candidate acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause harm to the Company. The Company may seek appropriate legal remedies, including injunctive relief.

### 10. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the **[Governing Law: State of New York]**, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

### 11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

(a) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality during the recruitment process.
(b) **Severability.** If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
