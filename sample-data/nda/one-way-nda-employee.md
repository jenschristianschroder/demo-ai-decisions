## NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (Employee / Contractor / Consultant)

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**[Company Name]**, a [jurisdiction] corporation with its principal place of business at [address] ("Company"), and

**[Individual Name]**, an individual residing at [address] ("Recipient").

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Company wishes to disclose certain Confidential Information to the Recipient in connection with the Recipient's engagement as a **[employee / contractor / consultant]** working on **[Project/Department Description]** (the "Purpose").

### 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information of the Company disclosed to the Recipient during the course of engagement, including but not limited to:

- Trade secrets, know-how, and proprietary processes
- Source code, algorithms, and technical documentation
- Product designs, roadmaps, and development plans
- Customer lists, data, and relationships
- Financial information, pricing strategies, and business plans
- Marketing strategies and competitive intelligence
- Employee compensation data and organizational information
- Information received from third parties under obligations of confidentiality
- Any information the Recipient knows or reasonably should know is confidential

### 3. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient;
(b) Was already known to the Recipient prior to employment/engagement, as documented in writing;
(c) Is independently developed by the Recipient outside the scope of engagement and without use of Confidential Information;
(d) Is required to be disclosed by law or regulation, provided the Recipient gives prompt notice to the Company.

### 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECIPIENT

The Recipient shall:

(a) Use the Confidential Information solely for performing duties related to the Purpose;
(b) Not disclose the Confidential Information to any person outside the Company without prior written authorization;
(c) Not remove Confidential Information from Company premises or systems except as required to perform duties;
(d) Comply with all Company information security policies and procedures;
(e) Promptly report any unauthorized disclosure or security incident to the Company;
(f) Not use Confidential Information for personal benefit or for the benefit of any third party.

### 5. INVENTION ASSIGNMENT REFERENCE

The Recipient acknowledges that inventions, works of authorship, and discoveries created during the course of engagement may be subject to a separate Invention Assignment Agreement or employment agreement. This NDA does not supersede any such agreements.

### 6. NON-SOLICITATION REFERENCE

The Recipient acknowledges that non-solicitation obligations may be set forth in a separate employment agreement or consulting agreement. This NDA does not create or modify any such obligations.

### 7. TERM

(a) **Trade Secrets:** The Recipient's obligations with respect to information that constitutes a trade secret under applicable law shall continue for as long as the information remains a trade secret — **indefinite duration**.
(b) **Other Confidential Information:** The Recipient's obligations with respect to all other Confidential Information shall continue for a period of **[Term: 3 years]** following the termination of the Recipient's engagement with the Company.

### 8. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon termination of engagement with the Company, or upon request by the Company, the Recipient shall:

(a) Immediately return all Confidential Information in any form, including documents, files, devices, and media;
(b) Permanently delete all Confidential Information from personal devices, cloud storage, and email accounts;
(c) Not retain any copies, summaries, or extracts of the Confidential Information;
(d) Confirm compliance in writing.

### 9. REMEDIES

The Recipient acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Company. Accordingly, the Company shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity.

### 10. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **[Governing Law: State of New York]**, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

### 11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

(a) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality between the Company and the Recipient, unless superseded by a separate employment or consulting agreement.
(b) **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.
(c) **Severability.** If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
(d) **Survival.** The obligations of this Agreement shall survive termination of the Recipient's engagement.
