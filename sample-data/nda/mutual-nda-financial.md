## MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT — FINANCIAL DUE DILIGENCE

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[Effective Date]** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**[Party A Name]**, a [jurisdiction] corporation with its principal place of business at [address] ("Party A"), and

**[Party B Name]**, a [jurisdiction] corporation with its principal place of business at [address] ("Party B").

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential **[merger / acquisition / investment / strategic partnership / joint venture]** (the "Transaction") and, in connection therewith, each Party may disclose certain Confidential Information, including financial data, projections, and material non-public information, to the other Party.

### 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by either Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"), including but not limited to:

- **Financial statements**, audited and unaudited, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements
- **Financial projections**, forecasts, models, and business plans
- **Valuation analyses**, capitalization tables, and equity structures
- **Revenue data**, EBITDA, margins, and unit economics
- **Customer contracts**, pipeline data, and revenue concentration information
- **Debt instruments**, credit agreements, and material liabilities
- **Intellectual property** portfolios and valuations
- **Material non-public information** ("MNPI") as defined under applicable securities laws
- **Employee compensation** data, organizational structures, and key personnel information
- **Regulatory filings**, compliance data, and pending litigation details
- Any information marked as "Confidential," "Proprietary," or "Restricted"

### 3. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by contemporaneous written records;
(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information;
(d) Is lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction on disclosure and without breach of any duty of confidentiality;
(e) Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice (at least 10 business days where permissible) to the Disclosing Party and cooperates with any effort to obtain protective treatment.

### 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party shall:

(a) Use the Confidential Information solely for evaluating the Transaction;
(b) Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, except to its officers, directors, employees, legal counsel, accountants, and financial advisors (collectively, "Representatives") who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those herein;
(c) Be responsible for any breach of this Agreement by its Representatives;
(d) Not use any MNPI in connection with the purchase or sale of any securities in violation of applicable securities laws;
(e) Protect the Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own most sensitive confidential information;
(f) Not contact any customers, suppliers, employees, or agents of the Disclosing Party regarding the Transaction without prior written consent.

### 5. STANDSTILL PROVISION

For a period of **[Standstill Period: 12 months]** from the Effective Date, neither Party shall, without the prior written consent of the other Party:

(a) Acquire or seek to acquire any securities or assets of the other Party;
(b) Propose or seek to effect any merger, business combination, or other extraordinary transaction involving the other Party;
(c) Solicit proxies or consents from the shareholders of the other Party;
(d) Form, join, or participate in a "group" (within the meaning of Section 13(d)(3) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) with respect to any securities of the other Party.

### 6. SECURITIES LAW COMPLIANCE

Each Party acknowledges that the Confidential Information may contain MNPI. Each Party agrees that it will not trade in the securities of the other Party on the basis of MNPI received under this Agreement, and will comply with all applicable securities laws, including but not limited to the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and Regulation FD.

### 7. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **[Term: 3 years]** from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated by either Party upon thirty (30) days' written notice. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for a period of **[Survival Period: 3 years]** following the date of termination or expiration.

### 8. RESIDUALS

The residuals clause is **intentionally excluded** from this Agreement. No residuals rights are granted with respect to financial Confidential Information, MNPI, or any information related to the Transaction.

### 9. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF MATERIALS

Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, upon the decision not to proceed with the Transaction, or upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall within **ten (10) business days**:

(a) Return or securely destroy all Confidential Information, including all copies, notes, analyses, compilations, and derivatives prepared by the Receiving Party or its Representatives;
(b) Provide a written certification of destruction signed by an authorized officer;
(c) **Exception:** The Receiving Party may retain copies required by applicable law, regulation, or internal compliance policies, provided such copies remain subject to the confidentiality obligations herein.

### 10. NO LICENSE OR WARRANTY

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any rights, license, or interest in the Confidential Information. ALL CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION IS PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING ANY WARRANTY OF ACCURACY OR COMPLETENESS.

### 11. NO OBLIGATION TO PROCEED

Nothing in this Agreement obligates either Party to proceed with the Transaction or to enter into any further agreement. Either Party may terminate discussions at any time without liability.

### 12. REMEDIES

The Parties acknowledge that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate. Accordingly, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, without the necessity of proving actual damages or posting a bond, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity.

### 13. INDEMNIFICATION

The Receiving Party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Disclosing Party from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from any breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party or its Representatives, including any violation of applicable securities laws.

### 14. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **[Governing Law: State of Delaware]**, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in **[Jurisdiction: Wilmington, DE]**.

### 15. GENERAL PROVISIONS

(a) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the subject matter hereof.
(b) **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both Parties.
(c) **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party.
(d) **Severability.** If any provision is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
(e) **Waiver.** No waiver of any provision shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the waiving Party.
