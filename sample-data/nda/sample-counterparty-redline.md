# Sample Counterparty Redline — Mutual NDA

**Context:** Acme Corp's legal team has returned a redlined version of our standard Mutual NDA — General template. The following shows the proposed changes with annotations.

## MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **January 15, 2026** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**Contoso Ltd.**, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 100 Innovation Drive, Seattle, WA 98101 ("Contoso"), and

**Acme Corp.**, a California corporation with its principal place of business at 500 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Acme").

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential technology partnership concerning cloud platform integration and API development (the "Purpose") and, in connection therewith, each Party may disclose certain Confidential Information to the other Party.

### 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by either Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"), whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by inspection, including but not limited to:

- Business plans, strategies, and projections
- Customer and supplier lists
- Financial information and pricing
- Marketing plans and data
- Technical information, know-how, and trade secrets
- Product designs and specifications
- **[ADDED by Acme]** Source code, algorithms, and software architecture
- **[ADDED by Acme]** Employee compensation data and organizational charts
- Any information marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"
- **[ADDED by Acme]** Any information disclosed orally that is identified as confidential at the time of disclosure and confirmed in writing within 30 days

### 3. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; **[MODIFIED by Acme: removed "as evidenced by written records"]**
(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information;
(d) Is lawfully obtained from a third party without restriction on disclosure;
(e) Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order. **[MODIFIED by Acme: removed "provided the Receiving Party gives prompt notice to the Disclosing Party"]**

### 4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party shall:

(a) Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
(b) Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party;
(c) Limit access to Confidential Information to its employees, agents, **contractors, and affiliated companies** **[MODIFIED by Acme: added "contractors and affiliated companies"]** who have a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those herein;
(d) Protect the Confidential Information with **commercially reasonable care** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from "at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care"]**;
(e) Promptly notify the Disclosing Party of any unauthorized disclosure or use of the Confidential Information.

### 5. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **5 years** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from 2 years to 5 years]** from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated by either Party upon **sixty (60) days'** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from 30 days to 60 days]** written notice. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for a period of **1 year** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from 2 years to 1 year]** following the date of termination or expiration.

### 6. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF MATERIALS

Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, or upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall **within thirty (30) business days** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from "promptly"]** return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession, including all copies, extracts, and summaries thereof. **[MODIFIED by Acme: removed certification requirement]**

**[ADDED by Acme]** Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Receiving Party may retain one archival copy of the Confidential Information for legal compliance purposes, subject to the ongoing confidentiality obligations herein.

### 7. NO LICENSE OR WARRANTY

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any rights or license in the Confidential Information except as expressly set forth herein. ALL CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION IS PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND.

### 8. REMEDIES

The Parties acknowledge that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate. **[MODIFIED by Acme: removed "Accordingly, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity." and replaced with:]**

**[ADDED by Acme]** In the event of a breach, the non-breaching Party's sole and exclusive remedy shall be to seek monetary damages, which shall in no event exceed the greater of (a) $100,000 or (b) actual documented damages.

### 9. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of California** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from New York to California]**, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved by **binding arbitration in San Francisco, CA under the rules of the American Arbitration Association** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from court jurisdiction to binding arbitration]**.

### 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

(a) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements.
(b) **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both Parties.
(c) **Assignment.** **Either Party may assign this Agreement to an affiliate or successor entity without the consent of the other Party.** **[MODIFIED by Acme: changed from requiring prior written consent]**
(d) **Severability.** If any provision is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
(e) **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.

**[ADDED by Acme — New Section]**

### 11. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of one (1) year following its termination, neither Party shall directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or hire any employee of the other Party who was involved in the activities contemplated by this Agreement, without the prior written consent of the other Party.
