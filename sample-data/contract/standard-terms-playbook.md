# Legal Department Playbook — Standard Contract Terms

## Overview

This playbook contains the standard contractual positions and approved clause language for Maersk Global Logistics A/S. All vendor agreements must be reviewed against these standards. Deviations require approval from the Legal Department.

---

## 1. Liability Cap

**Standard Position:** The vendor's total aggregate liability shall not exceed TWO TIMES (2x) the total fees paid or payable under the agreement in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

**Rationale:** A 2x multiplier provides adequate protection for service failures while remaining commercially reasonable.

**Red Lines:**
- Liability caps below 1x annual fees are NOT acceptable
- Unlimited liability clauses for the client are NOT acceptable
- Liability caps that exclude data breach or IP infringement are NOT acceptable

---

## 2. Indemnification

**Standard Position:** Mutual indemnification with carve-outs for:
- Vendor: IP infringement, data breach, gross negligence, willful misconduct
- Client: Misuse of services, violation of law

**Requirements:**
- Indemnification must be uncapped for data breaches involving personal data
- IP infringement indemnity must cover all costs including legal fees
- Vendor must indemnify for subcontractor actions

**Red Lines:**
- One-sided indemnification favoring the vendor is NOT acceptable
- Caps on data breach indemnification are NOT acceptable

---

## 3. Termination

**Standard Position:**
- Termination for convenience: 60 days notice (not 90 days)
- Termination for cause: 15 days cure period for material breach (not 30 days)
- Transition assistance: minimum 12 months at no additional cost

**Requirements:**
- Client must have right to terminate for convenience
- Cure period should not exceed 15 business days
- Transition assistance period must be at least 12 months
- Transition assistance must be at no additional cost or at-cost basis

**Red Lines:**
- Termination for convenience requiring more than 60 days notice
- Cure periods exceeding 30 days
- No transition assistance provision

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**Standard Position:**
- Payment within 60 days of invoice receipt (NET 60)
- Late payment interest: 1% per month (not 2%)
- No right to suspend services for late payment

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 60-day payment terms
- Late interest should not exceed legal maximum or 1.5% per month
- Service suspension only after 90+ days and with 30 days written notice

**Red Lines:**
- Payment terms shorter than 30 days
- Late interest exceeding 2% per month
- Immediate suspension rights for late payment

---

## 5. Confidentiality

**Standard Position:**
- Mutual confidentiality obligations
- Duration: 7 years post-disclosure (not 5 years)
- Surviving termination of the agreement

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 7-year confidentiality period
- Must survive termination
- Must cover all subcontractors
- Return/destruction of confidential information within 15 days of request

**Red Lines:**
- Confidentiality periods less than 5 years
- No obligation to destroy/return information
- Carve-outs for "business partners" without written consent

---

## 6. Data Protection

**Standard Position:**
- Full GDPR compliance required
- No cross-border transfers without Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) as appendix
- Right to audit vendor's data processing
- Breach notification within 24 hours (not 72 hours)
- Data deletion within 15 days of termination (not 30 days)

**Requirements:**
- Written DPA required as part of agreement
- Breach notification within 24 hours
- Annual right to audit or SOC 2 Type II report
- Sub-processor approval rights
- Data localization within EU/EEA unless explicitly approved

**Red Lines:**
- No DPA or GDPR compliance commitment
- Breach notification exceeding 48 hours
- No audit rights
- Unrestricted sub-processor use
- Data processing outside EU/EEA without SCCs

---

## 7. Intellectual Property

**Standard Position:**
- All bespoke materials developed for Maersk: full ownership vests in Maersk
- Vendor pre-existing IP: perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license
- No assignment of Maersk IP to vendor
- Source code escrow for critical software

**Requirements:**
- Clear ownership of bespoke deliverables
- License to pre-existing IP must be perpetual and irrevocable
- Source code escrow for mission-critical systems
- No restrictions on Maersk using deliverables after termination

**Red Lines:**
- Vendor retaining ownership of bespoke deliverables
- Restrictive or revocable licenses to embedded IP
- No source code escrow for critical systems

---

## 8. Force Majeure

**Standard Position:**
- Standard force majeure clause
- Notification within 48 hours (not 5 business days)
- Termination right after 30 days (not 90 days)
- No automatic extension of contract term

**Requirements:**
- Prompt notification (within 48 hours)
- Right to terminate after 30 days of continuous force majeure
- No obligation to pay during force majeure suspension
- Vendor must maintain business continuity/disaster recovery plans

**Red Lines:**
- Force majeure extending beyond 60 days without termination right
- Continued payment obligations during force majeure
- Overly broad definition including economic hardship

---

## 9. Dispute Resolution

**Standard Position:**
- Governing law: Danish law (not Singapore law)
- Jurisdiction: Copenhagen, Denmark
- Escalation: Senior management negotiation → mediation → arbitration
- Arbitration seat: Copenhagen under ICC Rules

**Requirements:**
- Governing law must be Danish or English law
- Arbitration seat in Europe
- Escalation mechanism before formal proceedings
- Language: English

**Red Lines:**
- Governing law outside EU/UK
- Exclusive jurisdiction in vendor's country
- No escalation mechanism

---

## 10. Insurance

**Standard Position:**
- Professional indemnity: minimum USD 10,000,000 (not 5,000,000)
- Cyber liability: minimum USD 20,000,000 (not 10,000,000)
- General liability: minimum USD 5,000,000
- Must name Maersk as additional insured

**Requirements:**
- All policies must be maintained throughout the agreement term plus 12 months
- Certificates of insurance required annually
- 30-day notice of cancellation or material change
- Maersk named as additional insured

**Red Lines:**
- Professional indemnity below USD 5,000,000
- No cyber liability insurance
- Policies that can be cancelled without notice to Maersk

---

## 11. Service Levels

**Standard Position:**
- System availability: 99.9% (not 99.5%)
- Critical incident response: 10 minutes (not 15)
- Service credits for missed SLAs
- Right to terminate for persistent SLA failures

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 99.9% uptime for critical services
- Service credits must be meaningful (minimum 5% of monthly fee per breach)
- Persistent failure (3+ months) triggers termination right
- Monthly SLA reporting

**Red Lines:**
- Availability below 99.5%
- No service credits
- No termination right for persistent SLA failures

---

## 12. Compliance and Anti-Corruption

**Standard Position:**
- Full compliance with applicable laws
- Anti-bribery/anti-corruption representations and warranties
- Right to audit compliance
- Immediate termination for compliance violations

**Requirements:**
- Compliance with Maersk Supplier Code of Conduct
- Annual compliance certification
- Whistleblower mechanism
- Supply chain due diligence

**Red Lines:**
- No anti-corruption commitment
- No audit rights
- No termination right for compliance violations
