# Clause Library — Approved Alternative Language

This library contains pre-approved clause language that can be proposed as alternatives during contract negotiation.

---

## CL-001: Liability Cap (Standard)

> The Vendor's total aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), breach of statutory duty, or otherwise, shall not exceed an amount equal to two (2) times the total fees paid or payable by the Client under this Agreement during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the liability.

---

## CL-002: Liability Cap (Enhanced for Critical Services)

> The Vendor's total aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed an amount equal to three (3) times the total fees paid or payable by the Client under this Agreement during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the liability. This cap shall not apply to: (a) liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence; (b) fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation; (c) breaches of data protection obligations; or (d) infringement of intellectual property rights.

---

## CL-003: Termination for Convenience

> Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason or no reason upon not less than sixty (60) calendar days' prior written notice to the other party. Upon such termination, the Vendor shall: (a) cease all Services on the termination date; (b) deliver all work product completed to date; (c) provide transition assistance as set forth in Section [X]; and (d) refund any pre-paid fees for Services not yet rendered.

---

## CL-004: Termination for Cause

> Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party: (a) commits a material breach of this Agreement that is not remedied within fifteen (15) business days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach; (b) becomes insolvent, enters administration, or is subject to any analogous proceedings; or (c) fails to comply with applicable anti-bribery, anti-corruption, or sanctions laws.

---

## CL-005: Transition Assistance

> Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason, the Vendor shall provide transition assistance services for a minimum period of twelve (12) months at no additional cost to the Client. Such services shall include: (a) knowledge transfer to the Client or its designated successor; (b) continued provision of Services during the transition period; (c) export of all Client data in a format specified by the Client; (d) destruction of all Client Confidential Information upon completion of transition; and (e) reasonable cooperation with any successor vendor.

---

## CL-006: Payment Terms (NET 60)

> The Client shall pay all undisputed invoices within sixty (60) calendar days of receipt of a valid invoice. Invoices must contain sufficient detail to allow verification. Late payment of undisputed amounts shall bear interest at a rate of one percent (1%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower. The Vendor shall have no right to suspend or withhold Services for non-payment unless payment is more than ninety (90) days overdue and the Vendor has provided at least thirty (30) days' prior written notice of its intention to suspend.

---

## CL-007: Confidentiality

> Each party (the "Receiving Party") shall maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information received from the other party (the "Disclosing Party") for a period of seven (7) years following disclosure, and shall not use such information for any purpose other than performing its obligations or exercising its rights under this Agreement. The Receiving Party shall protect Confidential Information with the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information, but in no event less than a reasonable standard of care. Upon request or termination, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information within fifteen (15) days and certify such return or destruction in writing.

---

## CL-008: Data Protection (GDPR Standard)

> The Vendor shall process personal data solely on behalf of the Client and in accordance with: (a) the Client's documented instructions; (b) the Data Processing Agreement set forth in Appendix [X]; and (c) all applicable data protection laws, including Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR). The Vendor shall: (i) notify the Client of any personal data breach within twenty-four (24) hours of discovery; (ii) not transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area without appropriate safeguards under Chapter V of the GDPR and the Client's prior written consent; (iii) submit to audits upon reasonable notice; (iv) maintain records of processing activities; and (v) delete or return all personal data within fifteen (15) days of termination.

---

## CL-009: Intellectual Property Assignment

> All Intellectual Property Rights in any materials, deliverables, or work product created by the Vendor specifically for the Client pursuant to this Agreement ("Bespoke Materials") shall vest in the Client absolutely upon creation. The Vendor hereby assigns (and shall procure that its personnel assign) all such rights to the Client. The Vendor shall execute all documents reasonably requested to perfect such assignment. The Vendor grants the Client a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license to use any of the Vendor's pre-existing intellectual property that is embedded in or necessary for the use of the Bespoke Materials.

---

## CL-010: Force Majeure

> Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this Agreement to the extent that such failure or delay is caused by a Force Majeure Event. The affected party shall: (a) notify the other party in writing within forty-eight (48) hours of becoming aware of the Force Majeure Event; (b) use reasonable endeavours to mitigate the effects; and (c) resume performance as soon as reasonably practicable. If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than thirty (30) consecutive days, either party may terminate this Agreement by written notice without liability. No fees shall be payable in respect of Services not provided during the Force Majeure Event.

## CL-011: Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

> This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Denmark, without regard to its conflict of laws principles. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved as follows: (a) the parties shall first attempt to resolve the dispute through good-faith negotiation between senior executives for a period of thirty (30) days; (b) if negotiation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to mediation under the rules of the Danish Institute of Arbitration; (c) if mediation fails within sixty (60) days, the dispute shall be finally resolved by arbitration under the ICC Rules, with three (3) arbitrators, seated in Copenhagen, Denmark. The language of the proceedings shall be English.

---

## CL-012: Insurance Requirements

> The Vendor shall, at its own expense, maintain throughout the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months thereafter, the following insurance policies: (a) Professional Indemnity Insurance with a minimum coverage of USD 10,000,000 per occurrence and in aggregate; (b) Cyber Liability Insurance with a minimum coverage of USD 20,000,000 per occurrence and in aggregate; (c) Commercial General Liability Insurance with a minimum coverage of USD 5,000,000 per occurrence. The Client shall be named as an additional insured on all policies. The Vendor shall provide certificates of insurance annually and shall provide thirty (30) days' written notice of any cancellation or material change to coverage.

---

## CL-013: Service Level Credits

> In the event the Vendor fails to meet any Service Level set forth in Schedule [X], the Client shall be entitled to service credits calculated as follows: (a) for each percentage point (or part thereof) below the target availability, a credit of five percent (5%) of the applicable monthly fee; (b) for each response time breach, a credit of two percent (2%) of the applicable monthly fee; subject to a maximum aggregate credit of thirty percent (30%) of the monthly fee in any given month. Service credits shall be applied against the next invoice. If the Vendor fails to meet any Service Level for three (3) or more consecutive months, the Client may terminate this Agreement for cause.

---

## CL-014: Anti-Corruption

> The Vendor represents, warrants, and undertakes that: (a) it shall comply with all applicable anti-bribery and anti-corruption laws, including the UK Bribery Act 2010 and the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act; (b) it has not and shall not offer, promise, give, or authorize any bribe, kickback, or other improper payment; (c) it shall maintain adequate procedures to prevent bribery; (d) it shall promptly report any suspected violation; and (e) it shall provide an annual compliance certification. Any breach of this clause shall constitute a material breach entitling the Client to terminate this Agreement immediately without liability.

---

## CL-015: Audit Rights

> The Client shall have the right, upon thirty (30) days' written notice and no more than once per calendar year (unless a material concern is identified), to audit or have audited the Vendor's facilities, systems, records, and processes relevant to the provision of the Services and compliance with this Agreement. The Vendor shall cooperate fully with any such audit and provide all reasonable access and assistance. As an alternative, the Vendor may provide a current SOC 2 Type II report from a recognized auditor covering the relevant controls.
