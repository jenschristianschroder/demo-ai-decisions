## 1. Introduction and Background

Acme Public Services is a regional government agency serving 2.4 million residents across 12 counties. We currently use a legacy reporting system that is approaching end-of-life. We are seeking a modern enterprise analytics and reporting platform to replace our current system and enable data-driven decision-making across all departments.

## 4. Product Capabilities

### 4.1 Core Analytics Requirements
- REQ-PC-001: The platform must support interactive dashboards with drill-down capabilities.
- REQ-PC-002: The platform must support ad-hoc query building for non-technical users.
- REQ-PC-003: The platform must provide scheduled report generation and distribution via email.
- REQ-PC-004: The platform must support at least 500 concurrent users without performance degradation.
- REQ-PC-005: The platform must provide role-based access control for dashboard and report access.
- REQ-PC-006: The platform should support natural language query capabilities. (Optional)
- REQ-PC-007: The platform must support embedding analytics into third-party applications via API or iframe.
- REQ-PC-008: The platform must support real-time data refresh with latency under 5 minutes for operational dashboards.

### 4.2 Visualization
- REQ-VZ-001: The platform must support standard chart types including bar, line, pie, scatter, heatmap, and geographic/map visualizations.
- REQ-VZ-002: The platform must support custom color themes and white-labeling with Acme branding.

---

## 5. Data Integrations

- REQ-DI-001: The platform must integrate with Microsoft SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and Oracle databases via native connectors.
- REQ-DI-002: The platform must support REST API data ingestion.
- REQ-DI-003: The platform must support flat file import (CSV, Excel).
- REQ-DI-004: The platform must provide an ETL/ELT capability or integrate with existing ETL tools (e.g., Azure Data Factory, SSIS).
- REQ-DI-005: The platform should support real-time streaming data sources. (Optional)
- REQ-DI-006: The platform must provide a data catalog or metadata management capability.

---

## 6. Security Requirements

- REQ-SEC-001: The platform must support SAML 2.0 and/or OpenID Connect for single sign-on.
- REQ-SEC-002: The vendor must maintain SOC 2 Type II certification.
- REQ-SEC-003: The platform must encrypt data at rest using AES-256 or equivalent.
- REQ-SEC-004: The platform must encrypt data in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
- REQ-SEC-005: The platform must support multi-factor authentication.
- REQ-SEC-006: The vendor must provide annual penetration test results upon request.
- REQ-SEC-007: The vendor must notify Acme of any security breaches within 24 hours.
- REQ-SEC-008: The platform must maintain audit logs for all user activities for a minimum of 2 years.
- REQ-SEC-009: The vendor must allow Acme to conduct security audits of the platform and infrastructure upon reasonable notice.

---

## 7. Privacy and Compliance

- REQ-PRV-001: The platform must comply with state and federal data privacy regulations applicable to government entities.
- REQ-PRV-002: The vendor must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
- REQ-PRV-003: The platform must support data anonymization and masking for sensitive fields.
- REQ-PRV-004: The platform must provide configurable data retention and deletion policies.
- REQ-PRV-005: The vendor must comply with CJIS requirements for any criminal justice data.

---

## 8. Implementation

- REQ-IMP-001: The vendor must provide a detailed implementation plan with milestones and deliverables.
- REQ-IMP-002: Implementation must be completed within 6 months of contract signing.
- REQ-IMP-003: The vendor must provide data migration support from the legacy system (Cognos-based).
- REQ-IMP-004: The vendor must provide on-site and remote training for up to 50 administrators and 200 end users.
- REQ-IMP-005: The vendor must provide a dedicated project manager for the implementation period.
- REQ-IMP-006: The implementation timeline must include penalties for missed milestone dates exceeding 30 days.

---

## 9. Support and SLA

- REQ-SLA-001: The vendor must provide 24/7 support for Severity 1 (system down) issues.
- REQ-SLA-002: The platform must maintain 99.9% uptime measured monthly.
- REQ-SLA-003: Response time for Severity 1 issues must be within 30 minutes.
- REQ-SLA-004: Response time for Severity 2 issues must be within 4 hours.
- REQ-SLA-005: The vendor must provide service credits of 5% of monthly fees for each 0.1% below the 99.9% uptime SLA.
- REQ-SLA-006: The vendor must provide a customer success manager assigned to Acme.

---

## 10. Pricing

- REQ-PRC-001: Provide pricing for 500 named users and 100 concurrent users.
- REQ-PRC-002: Pricing must include all licensing, implementation, training, and first-year support costs.
- REQ-PRC-003: Provide pricing for a 3-year and 5-year contract term.
- REQ-PRC-004: The vendor must guarantee most-favored-customer pricing for government clients.
- REQ-PRC-005: Pricing must be provided using the attached Pricing Template.

---

## 11. Legal and Contractual Terms

- REQ-LEG-001: The vendor must accept unlimited liability for data breaches involving Acme data.
- REQ-LEG-002: The vendor must provide source code escrow for the platform.
- REQ-LEG-003: The contract must include a termination for convenience clause with 90 days notice.
- REQ-LEG-004: The vendor must maintain cyber liability insurance of at least $10 million.
- REQ-LEG-005: The vendor must agree to uncapped indemnification for intellectual property infringement claims.
- REQ-LEG-006: Disputes must be resolved under the laws of the state where Acme is headquartered.

---

## 12. Accessibility

- REQ-ACC-001: The platform must comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility standards.
- REQ-ACC-002: The platform must support screen readers and keyboard-only navigation.
- REQ-ACC-003: The vendor must provide a Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT).

---

## 13. Reporting and Dashboards

- REQ-RPT-001: The platform must include at least 20 pre-built dashboard templates for common government use cases.
- REQ-RPT-002: The platform must support pixel-perfect formatted reports for printing and PDF export.
- REQ-RPT-003: The platform must support scheduled report delivery to distribution lists.

---

## 14. Data Residency

- REQ-DR-001: All data must be stored within the continental United States.
- REQ-DR-002: The vendor must commit to not transferring Acme data outside the United States without prior written consent.
- REQ-DR-003: The vendor must specify the exact data center locations used for hosting Acme data.

- REQ-REF-002: At least one reference must be from an organization with over 1 million constituents served.
- REQ-REF-003: References must include contact information and project scope summary.
